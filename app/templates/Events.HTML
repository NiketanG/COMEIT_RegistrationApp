Clash of Code
The objective of this event is to provide a platform for young engineers to challenge other participants and test their competitive programming knowledge.

Structure :
Round 1: MCQ’s
In first round, you have to solve simple MCQ’s based on basic knowledge of C or C++ programming language. You have to solve these questions in team.
This round is of 15 minutes with questions of different difficulty level. The scores associated with each question differs according to its difficulty level. Top scores will qualify for next round.
Round 2: Problem Solving
In second round, a problem statement given. Both members will have to solve the problem together. The teams providing correct solution with least time will be selected for final round.
Round 3 :
You will be given two problem statements. Each member has to solve one problem statement at a time on different PCs.
After half time both members will be swapped and have to solve other team member’s problem statement.
First group who will solve both the problem statement will be the winner of Clash of Codes. Runner up would also be awarded.

Rules :
1. Participants need to bring their College ID cards and the receipt of registration during reporting.
2. Time slots will be given and the participants are expected to follow it strictly.
3. Participants are expected to give their correct contact details, so as to inform them about the results. 4. Further details of the round will be disclosed at the time of event.
5. In case of any disputes, the decision of the organizers and the judges will be final and binding to all.
6. Team members may be from different colleges/institutions but one participant may not be a part of multiple teams for the same event. 7. Use of Mobile/Internet is not allowed.

Participants : 1 | 2
Entry Fee: Rs. 50/- per team
Prize: Winner: Rs 3000/- + Trophy and Runner-up: Rs 2000/- + Trophy

Hotkeys
The objective of this event is to test the participant’s basic knowledge of Shortcut Keys .This event is for programmers having experience to work without mouse.

Structure :
Round 1: MCQ’s on basic Hotkeys of Windows
This round will contain MCQ’s on basic Shortcut keys of Windows. You have to answer the questions in the given time (10 min). The one who gives maximum answers will be selected for the next round. No negative marking.
Round 2: Task based on Windows that perform action related to shortcut key
Round 2 round based on the shortcut key knowledge, shortcut keys will be provided. You have to implement the action related to shortcut key with given time. The team who implement the task in minimum time it will select for last round
Round 3: Task based on Windows without using Mouse
Round 3 will have few tasks based of performing operations on Window version. Contestant making use of mouse will be disqualified. The one who completes the task in the minimum time will be selected as winner.
